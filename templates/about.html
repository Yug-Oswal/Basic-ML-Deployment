The model used is Inception V3 provided by Keras. It is fine-tuned on a credible dataset provided by Lawrence Moroney, Lead Advocate AI - Google. The dataset is a balanced dataset and consists of 2000 training images of cats and dogs. It also contains 1000 images for validation. The model has 95% training accuracy and 97.3% validation accuracy.
